# Hi, I'm Zhipeng Liu (劉志鵬) 👋

Ph.D. student in **Artificial Intelligence** at the [Graduate School of Science and Engineering, University of Toyama](https://toyamaailab.github.io/), Japan.

🔬 **Research interests:** deep learning, medical image analysis, image segmentation, and **dendritic learning** — designing biologically-inspired neuron models (dendritic neuron module) and validating them across diverse classification, detection, and segmentation tasks.

🧠 My long-term focus is on building **effective and interpretable deep learning methods** and bringing them from research into real-world use.
